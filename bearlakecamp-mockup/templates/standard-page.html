Our Mission

Bear Lake Camp exists to be a Christian Ministry Center which challenges individuals toward maturity in Christ and to provide a positive environment through services and facilities.

Since 1948, Bear Lake Camp has been a place where lives are transformed. Nestled in the heart of Pennsylvania's wilderness, we offer a sacred space away from the distractions of daily life where campers, retreat guests, and families can encounter God in powerful ways.

Our ministry serves students from 6th grade through high school during summer camp sessions, church groups seeking retreat facilities year-round, and families looking to create lasting memories together. Through engaging Bible teaching, outdoor adventure, authentic worship, and Christ-centered community, we help people of all ages deepen their relationship with Jesus.

Our History

Bear Lake Camp was founded in 1948 by a group of local churches who shared a vision: to create a place where young people could experience the transforming power of Christ in an outdoor setting. What began as a simple summer Bible camp with rustic cabins and outdoor latrines has grown into a year-round ministry with modern facilities serving thousands of guests annually.

Key milestones in our history include:

  • 1948: Camp founded with first summer session serving 40 campers
  • 1952: Chapel constructed, becoming the spiritual heart of camp
  • 1967: Dining Hall expanded to accommodate growing retreat ministry
  • 1985: First high ropes course installed for adventure programming
  • 2003: Ministry Activity Center (gymnasium) completed
  • 2018: Major chapel renovation with updated sound/lighting systems
  • 2024: Celebrated 76 years of ministry with over 50,000 lives impacted

Throughout our history, one thing has remained constant: our commitment to pointing people to Jesus. While our facilities have been updated and our programs have evolved, our mission has never wavered.

Our Core Values

Everything we do at Bear Lake Camp is guided by these foundational values:

Christ-Centered

Jesus is the foundation of our ministry. All programming, teaching, and interactions point campers and guests to the person and work of Christ. We believe transformation happens not through activities or experiences alone, but through encountering the living God.

Biblically Grounded

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word and the ultimate authority for faith and life. Our teaching is expository, age-appropriate, and designed to help students understand how Scripture applies to their daily lives.

Relationally Focused

Spiritual growth happens in the context of authentic relationships. Our counselors are trained to build genuine connections with campers, creating safe spaces for vulnerability, questions, and discipleship.

Experientially Driven

We believe God speaks through creation and adventure. Whether it's conquering a fear on the high ropes course, experiencing awe during a lakeside sunrise, or serving others through work projects, we design experiences that reveal spiritual truths in tangible ways.

Generationally Minded

We're not just running programs—we're investing in the next generation of Christian leaders. Many of our summer staff are former campers who now pour into younger students, creating a multi-generational legacy of faith.

Accreditation & Affiliations

Bear Lake Camp is proud to be accredited by the American Camp Association (ACA), the leading authority on youth development and camp safety standards. ACA accreditation means we meet over 300 health, safety, and program quality standards reviewed by independent inspectors.

We are also members of:

  • Christian Camp and Conference Association (CCCA) - Connecting us to a network of Christ-centered camps committed to excellence in ministry
  • Evangelical Council for Financial Accountability (ECFA) - Ensuring transparency and integrity in our financial practices
  • Pennsylvania Camp Association - Collaborating with other camps across the state to advance outdoor ministry
