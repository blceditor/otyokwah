The Chapel

A Sacred Space for Worship

The Chapel at Bear Lake Camp is the spiritual heart of our ministry. This beautifully renovated worship space seats 150 people and features natural wood beams, large windows overlooking the forest, and a welcoming atmosphere that draws campers and retreat guests into God's presence.

Built in 1952 and fully renovated in 2018, the Chapel blends rustic camp charm with modern amenities. The sanctuary features a stage for speakers and worship bands, state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems, and flexible seating that can be reconfigured for different group sizes and worship styles.

Whether you're hosting a weekend retreat, summer camp worship gathering, or intimate prayer meeting, the Chapel provides a distraction-free environment where hearts are open to hear from God.
