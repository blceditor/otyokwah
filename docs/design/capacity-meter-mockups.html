Design Notes

  • Status thresholds: "Almost Full!" at 85%+, "Filling Fast" at 65%+, "Spots Available" below 65%.
  • Color coding: Red (high urgency, 85%+), amber/orange (moderate, 65-84%), BLC primary blue or green (plenty of room, below 65%).
  • Option 1 (Progress Bars) works best as a standalone section on a registration or summer camp overview page.
  • Option 2 (Donut Charts) is visually striking and works well for a homepage feature or dashboard widget.
  • Option 3 (Card Grid) is the most action-oriented, with built-in CTAs. Best for a dedicated registration page.
  • Option 4 (Badges) is the most flexible — can be used inline, in navigation, or alongside existing session listings.
  • Option 5 (Table) is the most information-dense. Great for parents comparing sessions side-by-side.
  • Recommendation: Options 3 and 4 can be combined — cards on the registration page, badges elsewhere on the site for at-a-glance availability.
