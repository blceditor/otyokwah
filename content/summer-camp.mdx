# Summer Camp 2026

**Life-changing experiences at Otyokwah**

## What to Expect

Summer camp at Otyokwah is more than just a week away from home. It's a place where students encounter Christ, build authentic friendships, and create memories that last a lifetime.

### Daily Schedule

**Morning**
- Worship and Bible study
- Small group discussions
- Prayer and reflection

**Afternoon**
- Swimming and water activities
- Sports and recreation
- Arts and crafts
- Free time

**Evening**
- Campfire worship
- Special activities
- Cabin time
- Devotions

## 2026 Summer Camp Dates

### Jr. High Camp (Grades 6-8)
- **Week 1:** June 14-20, 2026
- **Week 3:** June 28 - July 4, 2026

### High School Camp (Grades 9-12)
- **Week 2:** June 21-27, 2026
- **Week 4:** July 5-11, 2026

## What to Bring

- Bible and notebook
- Sleeping bag and pillow
- Toiletries and towels
- Comfortable clothes for activities
- Swimsuit and water shoes
- Flashlight
- Spending money (optional - camp store)
