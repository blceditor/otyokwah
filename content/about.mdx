# About Otyokwah

**Challenging individuals toward maturity in Christ since 1948**

## Our Mission

Otyokwah exists to be a Christian Ministry Center which challenges individuals toward maturity in Christ and to provide a positive environment through services and facilities.

We are committed to creating transformative experiences where campers encounter God, build lasting friendships, and grow in their faith journey.

## Our Core Values

### Christ-Centered
Every program and activity points campers and guests toward a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ.

### Community
We foster authentic Christian community where individuals can grow together in faith and fellowship.

### Excellence
We strive for excellence in all we do, honoring God through quality facilities, programs, and service.

## Our History

Otyokwah has been serving the Christian community for decades, providing a place where countless individuals have encountered Christ and grown in their faith.

From humble beginnings, the camp has grown to include modern facilities while maintaining the natural beauty and peaceful atmosphere that makes it a special place for spiritual growth.

Today, we continue the mission of challenging individuals toward maturity in Christ through summer camp programs, retreats, and ministry partnerships.
